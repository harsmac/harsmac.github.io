CLAD: Bias Mitigation

Contrastive Learning for Auditing and Debiasing

Project Summary

CLAD is a novel contrastive learning framework designed to mitigate background bias in datasets and models. By cleverly selecting positive and negative pairs, CLAD encourages representations that are invariant to spurious correlations while preserving the relevant signal for downstream tasks.

This methodology achieved state‑of‑the‑art results on several fairness benchmarks and contributed to a better understanding of how to design contrastive objectives for bias reduction.
